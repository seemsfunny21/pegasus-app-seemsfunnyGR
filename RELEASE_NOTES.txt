PEGASUS 234 - Mobile sync indicator raw-label lock

Problem fixed:
- The sync indicator was still appearing large/small because adaptiveTypography UI Label Polish was converting the text after countdown mutations.
- This caused alternating `ΣΥΝΔΕΔΕΜΕΝΟ` / `Συνδεδεμένο` visual states.

Changes:
- Added `data-pegasus-raw-label="true"` to `#sync-indicator`.
- `adaptiveTypography.js` now explicitly skips `#sync-indicator`.
- `mobile.html` re-applies the raw-label lock every countdown tick.
- `mobile/style.css` keeps the indicator dimensions fixed and prevents inherited child resizing.

Package:
- Code-only.
- No images folder.
- No videos folder.
